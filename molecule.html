This is a visualization of the pdb2rh1 molecule.

The structure is based on pdb2rh1.ent describing the molecule. 

          awk 'BEGIN {OFS=", "; print "var pdb2rh1_molecule = ["} /^ATOM/ {print "    { x: "substr($0, 32, 7),"y: "substr($0, 40, 7), "z: "substr($0, 48, 7), "element: \""substr($0, 78,1)"\" },"} END {print "];"}' pdb2rh1.ent > pdb2rh1.js
All atoms has been extracted by a simple awk command, and turned into a simple js list of atoms.

The atoms are color coded by element, C=gray, N=blue, O=red, S=yellow, H=white. All atoms with an euclidian distance of less than 1.9 has a bond drawn as a simple line.

The view is controlled by the mouse, Left click to rotate, middle click to zoom, right click to pan.
These controls are taken from the orbital controls example from three.js.
A black dot is drawn to indicate the pivot of the camera.

To accentuate details a simple lighting system has been put in place with light source at the camera and at the pivot to highlight the areas being looked at.
